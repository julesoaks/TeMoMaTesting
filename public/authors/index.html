Melissa Bowen

 Principal Investigator

Melissa Bowen is the principal investigator of the project. She is a physical oceanographer who works on understanding the ocean from the global overturning circulation to estuaries. Over the last several decades, her main research focus is understanding how the ocean around New Zealand is changing.

Mike Kittridge

 Research Fellow

I am a hydrologic and atmospheric scientist that is a strong proponent of scientific work being fully reproducible, easily testable, open-source, and extendable. I also design and build systems for managing large amounts of data, develop the scientific processing and analysis tools that sit on top of these systems to do the data science magic, and create the reporting tools to present the results in the most valuable way for users. I’ve had a varied career in the areas of clean(ish) freshwater science.
