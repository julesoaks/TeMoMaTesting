About the Project

Oceans affect everything around New Zealand, from local weather to the productivity of our industries. Yet, oceans in the region are changing significantly under climate change. We are a team of oceanographers, atmospheric, and social science experts who will assess:

  • Future coastal ocean temperatures and marine heat waves
  • Changes in extreme weather events due to the changing ocean
  • Coastal ocean thermal refugia and hot spots
  • Forecasting ocean surface temperatures
